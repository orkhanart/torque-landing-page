Color Philosophy

Deep Intelligence uses rich, dark backgrounds with subtle blue undertones (not pure black) to create depth and sophistication. The electric cyan accent conveys precision, clarity, and technological advancement without feeling cold or clinical.

This palette creates a sense of calm confidence - the interface knows what it's doing. The cool tones suggest objectivity and data-driven decision making, while the depth of the backgrounds implies there's always more beneath the surface.
