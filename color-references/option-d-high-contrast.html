High Contrast Signal

Neutral gray base with a bold, unexpected accent. The data is neutral - our insight cuts through like a signal in the noise.

Color Philosophy

High Contrast Signal treats the interface as a neutral data environment where one bold color becomes the brand's signature. The true neutral grays (neither warm nor cool) create perfect objectivity.

The Electric Mint accent (#00FFB2) is unexpected and memorable - not the typical blue or purple of tech. It signals action, insight, and opportunity against the quiet backdrop of data.

This approach makes every colored element meaningful: if it's mint, it matters.
