Color Philosophy

Warm Infrastructure uses dark backgrounds with subtle brown/stone undertones instead of pure black or blue. This creates a sense of solidity and earthiness - like premium leather or aged wood. The amber/gold accent adds energy and optimism without feeling cold or clinical.

This palette says "we're sophisticated technology, but we're built by humans, for humans." It's infrastructure you can trust because it feels substantial and real, not sterile and detached.
