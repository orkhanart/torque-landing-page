Color Philosophy

Pure black and white creates maximum clarity. No accent colors competing for attention. Every element earns its place through typography, spacing, and hierarchy alone.

Light mode signals transparency - nothing to hide. The data speaks for itself. This is infrastructure confidence: we don't need flashy colors because the product is the statement.

01 Black for action, interaction, and emphasis
02 White for space, clarity, and breathing room
03 Grays for hierarchy, not decoration
04 Typography and spacing do the heavy lifting
