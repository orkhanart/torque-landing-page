## TIPS FOR FAL.AI

1. **Use FLUX.2 [pro]** for final assets — best quality
2. **Use FLUX.2 [klein]** for rapid iterations — test prompts quickly
3. **Include HEX codes** for precise brand colors
4. **Structure prompts:** Subject → Environment → Style → Technical
5. **Negative prompts** (if supported): "no text, no watermark, no people"
6. **Aspect ratios:** Specify in Fal.ai settings, not prompt
7. **Upscale** final selections to 4K for production use
